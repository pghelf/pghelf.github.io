Thrillers

This is where you will find reviews of the mystery books I have read.

The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides

I thoroughly enjoyed this book. I read this book in one day because I just couldn't put it down. There is a huge twist that I did not see coming and the ending was perfect. The ending perfectly tied together all the elements of the story in a way that made my jaw drop.

Historical Fiction

The Nightingale by Kristin Hannah

This book was a story about two sisters trying to find a way to survive WWII in France. This book was an amazing tear-jerker. It was beautifully written and caused so many tears, but ended in a way that I was not expecting. It's a longer book, but it's well worth the read.
